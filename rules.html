Animal Memory Game
  • Rules (current)
The Animal Memory Card Game is a cognitive game designed to enhance your memory! The rules are as follows:
  • A set of 36 cards are faced down
  • There are 18 pairs of cards and each pair has the same animal picture on it.
  • Your job is to try your best to remember where the animal cards are and based on that, choose a match
  • Click on two cards to see if they match. If they don't, they face back down. If you get a pair, the cards face up for you!
